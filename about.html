About Us

Religence Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. is a pharmaceutical company focused on developing high-quality Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) to serve healthcare needs across India and globally. With robust manufacturing practices and a passion for innovation, we stand committed to regulatory compliance, quality, and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

We are committed to manufacturing safe, effective, and reliable Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) through cutting-edge technology, skilled personnel, and a deep-rooted culture of quality and integrity.

Leadership

Religence Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. is led by a team of seasoned professionals and technocrats with decades of experience in pharmaceutical R&D, manufacturing, and regulatory affairs. Our leadership ensures strategic growth, ethical compliance, and continuous excellence.
